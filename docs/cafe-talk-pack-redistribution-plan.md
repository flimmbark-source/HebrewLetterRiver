# Café Talk pack redistribution plan

This document turns the Café Talk cleanup discussion into an explicit pack plan.

## Core rules

- No Café Talk canonical word is allowed to become orphaned.
- Every Café Talk canonical word must belong to a pack.
- If a pack would become too large, split it into a new `02` / `03` pack.
- Café Talk should stay focused on conversation texture rather than carrying every common word by itself.
- Existing packs can keep their current beginner `bb-` words while the Café Talk canonical `bbct-` words get explicit ownership elsewhere.

## Notes for implementation

1. Stop generating runtime Café Talk packs directly from `CAFE_TALK_CATEGORIES`.
2. Define the new packs manually in `src/data/bridgeBuilderPacks.js`.
3. Update `src/data/bridgeBuilderSections.js` so the new packs appear in the appropriate sections.
4. Keep `src/data/readingTexts/cafeTalk/cafeTalkCanonical.js` as the canonical source of truth.
5. The app can keep existing beginner `bb-` word packs while also giving every canonical Café Talk `bbct-` word an explicit pack home.

## Why this plan exists

The current generated Café Talk section is too long and too repetitive. This plan keeps Café Talk itself short while making sure no canonical word disappears from the curriculum.
